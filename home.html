Metadata is a free online platform that allows access to various data of your files ranging from images, audio, videos and document. Metadata helps users to get access to all informations they can get from a file easily.

Who can benefit from Metadata

Photographers

it helps to add useful details such as copyright info, descriptions and also helps to categorise and protect images

Podcasters

Ensures greater accessibility to wide range of audience and also helps with keyword optimization

Videographers

Access to richer, automatically generated data, allowing greater use and more extensive filmmaking

Researchers & Others

Ensures proper reviewing of results and evaluating most relevant data
